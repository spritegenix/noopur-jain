Meet

Nooper

"A holistic healer, a mystic, a spiritual mentor and an ascension guide - I am here to open up a sacred path and guide you to the journey of healing and transformation."

“A path that truly aligns with your soul, is the one that will open doors you never knew existed”

How my path led to become a Healer and a Mystic?

As a child I grew up learning meditation, visiting temples with my family and sitting for hours doing meditation or reading ancient scriptures. This led my curious mind dive even deeper in to the mysteries of the universe and everything was behind the veil - the unexplored divine nature of our soul. As a result - at the age of 12, I went to the school of sacred and spiritual teachings to understand the path of our ascended masters. And, something shifted deep inside me that made me realise our multidimensional nature and our potential to expand

The missing link between our material and spiritual nature.

Eventually as I grew up, I gave my all into achieving success. I quickly climbed the ladder working with global brands and traveled around the world. Eventually as an established entrepreneur - I was exposed to some harsh realities of the world. The connection with our true divine essence was missing.

I started to feel the disconnect from my gifts and my ultimate purpose here.

There was no place for me to find answers and no-one to guide me along this path. And so, I moved to the divine land of Himalayas. There in parallel to my work I deeply immersed into my spiritual journey. Studied and practised under several traditions learning different healing modalities, yogic path and ancient meditation techniques.

My true gifts awaken.

Soon after, I realised my calling and awakened to my purpose. I now work with so many seekers and people who want to awaken this knowing within them - of their true nature and potential.

With the blessings of my ancestors and my parents who have been an immense support and guiding light for me - I am able to find my roots that helps me serve from the place of love. I wish for you to be inspired to create a life that is your highest expression and allow beautiful things to unfold.

Thank you for being here. If you’d like to connect, get in touch!
